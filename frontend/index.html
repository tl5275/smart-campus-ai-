Smart Campus AI Platform

AI-Driven Waste, Water, and Energy Operations for Campus Infrastructure

Smart Campus AI unifies bin telemetry, utility health, and digital twin simulation into one operational layer. Teams get real-time risk visibility, route optimization, and coordinated actions across zones.

Open Live Dashboard Analytics Digital Twin

Problem Statement

Campus operations are often siloed: waste, water, and energy incidents are tracked separately, slowing coordinated response.

Solution Architecture

FastAPI simulation + AI scoring + Leaflet command UI + OSRM road routing + zone-aware digital twin decision engine.

Operational Outcome

Proactive interventions, reduced overflow and leak risk, lower utility costs, and measurable sustainability improvements.

Platform Modules

  • Live Zone Monitoring: 100+ bins, water leak nodes, and energy overload signals.
  • AI Risk Scoring: Dynamic risk index blending critical bins and utility incidents.
  • Road Route Optimization: On-demand OSRM route generation for actionable waste pickups.
  • Digital Twin Simulation: Scenario, stress, and response-mode controls with live forecast impact.

Deployment-Ready Stack

  • Backend serves both APIs and frontend pages from a single FastAPI service.
  • Environment-driven settings for CORS, host, port, and runtime mode.
  • No localhost hardcoding in frontend; API calls use relative URLs.
  • Ready for Render/Railway with `Procfile` and `requirements.txt`.
